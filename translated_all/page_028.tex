\section*{General Solutions of Differential Equations}

We need to find general solutions of the differential equation \( Lu = 0 \).

\begin{align}
    UH - U_{xx} &= 0, \\
    3UH + 10U_x + 3U_{xx} &= 0.
\end{align}

Let \( v = (3 + 2)u \). Then we have:

\begin{align}
    30 + U_x &= 0, \\
    v &= f(3x - t), \\
    u &= g(3t - x) + H(3x - t).
\end{align}
